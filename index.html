Step-by-Step Custom Textile Creation

Discover our step-by-step approach to creating custom textile. We guide you through every stage, ensuring your vision is brought to life with precision and care.

1. Design Submission

Customers are allowed to submit their own designs or samples. Additionally, we can also develop a customized design in line with the client's concepts.

2. Custom Sample Creation

Based on the customer's design or sample, we will create a custom - made sample. .

3. Fabric Ordering

Once the client gives approval to the sample and makes the initial payment, we start ordering the bulk fabric.

4.Sample Shipment

We utilize the newly - ordered fabric to make another sample and send it to the client. This process is repeated until the client is completely satisfied.

5. Fabric Cutting

After the customer approves the sample, we move on to cut the fabric.

6. Production

When all the fabric has been cut, we commence mass production.

7. Quality Inspection

Our quality inspectors conduct strict checks on the finished products. Any defective items will be removed.

8. Packaging

We package the products according to the customer's requirements. Custom - packaging services are also provided.

9. Shipping

Once the packaging is finished, we arrange for the shipment. We provide various shipping options.
